HOW TO PLAY

🎯 CONTROLS:

• WASD/Arrow Keys: Move your ship

• Mouse: Aim direction

• Left Click: Fire primary weapon

• Right Click/Hold: Charge secondary weapon

• Shift/Space: Boost (limited fuel)

• Mouse Wheel: Change ammo type (Standard/Piercing/Explosive/Spread)

• I: Toggle inhibitor menu (in-game)

• M: Play/Pause music

• Tab: Skip song

• P/Esc: Pause game

⚡ GAMEPLAY:

• Your tail damages enemies - use it as a weapon!

• Bullets bounce off walls once before disappearing

• Bullet impacts create grid warp effects

• Avoid touching your own tail (causes damage)

• Different enemy types have unique behaviors

• Collect points to advance waves and levels

• Inhibitors give special powers and effects

• Allies fight alongside you (configure in menu)

🚀 TIPS:

• Use walls to ricochet shots around corners

• Your tail grows longer and more powerful over time

• Secondary weapon deals massive damage when charged

• Watch for bomber warnings - they explode!

• Change ammo types on the fly with mouse wheel

• Use 'I' key to adjust inhibitors mid-game
